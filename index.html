About Danny

Hammond Driven.
Rock Powered.

A Hammond organ driven rock keyboard player whose sound blends classic analogue power with modern stage instruments — Nord Stage IV, Korg and Kurzweil. Mentored by Jon Lord and Don Airey of Deep Purple. Performing with Absolute Bowie, Graham Bonnet Band and his own Rising From The Deep.
